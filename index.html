CHECKING OUT SOME OF

Spotify Audio Features

FEEL BLUE

VALENCE. A measure from 0.0 to 1.0 describing the musical positiveness conveyed by a track. High valence = more positive. Low valence = more negative.

FEEL LIKE DANCING

DANCEABILITY. How suitable a track is for dancing based on a combination of musical elements including tempo, rhythm stability, beat strength, and overall regularity. 0.0 = least danceable. 1.0 = most danceable.

FEEL THE ENERGY

ENERGY. 0.0 to 1.0 represents a perceptual measure of intensity and activity. Features include dynamic range, perceived loudness, timbre, onset rate, and general entropy. Energetic tracks feel fast, loud, and noisy. Death metal = high energy. Bach prelude = low energy.

The objective was to explore some of the Spotify audio features. The songs were randomly chosen and then categorized into four different playlists. A value above 0.5 was determined to qualify for a category, except "FEEL BLUE" which has a valence value of less than 0.5.

This is not an official Spotify project, but it is built with data from the open Spotify Web API.
